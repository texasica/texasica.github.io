About Us
Indian Cultural Association seeks to increase awareness about the South Asian heritage at The University of Texas at Austin. Founded on the three tenets of culture, community, and camaraderie, the Indian Cultural Association serves students as a cultural liaison to spread influence of the Indian ethnic identity. With various social, service, cultural, and professional events, our ultimate mission is to foster a strong appreciation for diversity and the preservation of the Indian culture on the campus and in the community as a whole.
